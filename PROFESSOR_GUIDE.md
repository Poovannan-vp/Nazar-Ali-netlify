# How to Update Your Website — Simple Guide

**For: Dr. A. Nazar Ali**

This website is built so that **you can update it yourself** without writing any code.

---

## Step 1: Open the Admin Panel

Open your browser and go to:

```
https://YOUR-WEBSITE-NAME.netlify.app/admin
```

*(Replace `YOUR-WEBSITE-NAME` with your actual domain. Your student will send you the exact link.)*

You'll see a login screen.

---

## Step 2: Log In

- First time: Click **"Sign up"** → enter your email → click the link in your email → set a password.
- After that: Just enter email + password.

---

## Step 3: You'll See These Sections

Once logged in, you'll see a left sidebar with:

- **🏠 Home (Hero Section)** — your name, title, bio, stats
- **👤 About Section** — bio paragraphs, expertise areas
- **🎓 Education** — degrees, universities, years
- **💼 Experience** — job positions
- **📚 Research & Publications** — citations, publications list
- **💡 Patents** — patents with optional images
- **🏆 Awards & Memberships** — awards with photos
- **📸 Gallery** — event photos (NEW!)
- **📬 Contact Info** — email, phone, address

Click any one to open it.

---

## Step 4: Common Tasks

### ➕ Add a New Award

1. Click **🏆 Awards & Memberships** on the left.
2. Scroll to the **Awards** list.
3. Click the **"Add Award"** button at the bottom of the list.
4. Fill in:
   - Award Title
   - Organization
   - Year
   - Icon (choose from dropdown)
   - Colour (choose from dropdown)
   - Description
   - **Award Photo** — click "Choose an Image" → upload from your computer.
5. Click **"Publish"** (top right) → **"Publish Now"**.
6. Wait 1–2 minutes. Refresh your website — done!

### 📸 Add Event Photos (Gallery)

1. Click **📸 Gallery**.
2. Scroll to **Photos**.
3. Click **"Add"**.
4. Upload the photo, add a caption, date, and category (Award / Guest Lecture / FDP / etc.).
5. Click **Publish** → **Publish Now**.

### ✏️ Edit Your Bio

1. Click **👤 About Section**.
2. Edit **Paragraph 1** or **Paragraph 2**.
3. Click **Publish** → **Publish Now**.

### 📞 Change Phone / Email

1. Click **📬 Contact Info**.
2. Update the Email or Phone.
3. Click **Publish** → **Publish Now**.

### 📚 Add a New Publication

1. Click **📚 Research & Publications**.
2. Scroll to **Publications**.
3. Click **Add**.
4. Fill in Title, Journal, Year, Type (SCI / Scopus), Citations, Impact Factor.
5. Click **Publish** → **Publish Now**.

### 💡 Add a New Patent

1. Click **💡 Patents**.
2. Click **Add** under Patents list.
3. Fill in details. Optionally upload the patent certificate image.
4. Click **Publish** → **Publish Now**.

### 🗑️ Delete an Entry

1. Open the section.
2. Find the item in the list.
3. Click the ❌ / delete button next to it.
4. Click **Publish** → **Publish Now**.

### 🔢 Reorder Items

Drag the drag-handle icon (⋮⋮) on the left of each item to reorder.

---

## Step 5: Always Click "Publish" Twice

After making any change:
1. First click **"Publish"** (top right).
2. Then click **"Publish Now"** in the menu.

⏱️ Your website will rebuild in about **1 minute**. Refresh to see changes.

---

## Tips

- ✅ **You can't break anything.** If you save a mistake, just edit it again.
- 📷 **Best photo size:** 1200×800 pixels (or any — it will auto-resize).
- 📱 **Use your phone too!** The admin panel works on mobile.
- 🌐 **Don't change URLs** unless you have a new Google Scholar / ORCID account.

---

## If Something Goes Wrong

Contact your student/developer. They can:
- Reset your password
- Fix the design (colors, layout)
- Add new sections
- Recover deleted content (everything is backed up in GitHub automatically)
